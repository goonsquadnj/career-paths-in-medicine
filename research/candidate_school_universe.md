# Candidate School Universe

This is the research pool for Lucy’s Healthcare Pathway Planner.

This is not the final school list.

The goal is to start with a broad but manageable universe of schools, perform a fast data sweep, and then select approximately 20–30 representative schools for v1 of the app.

The final app list should represent strategic categories, not pretend to rank all colleges.

# Selection rule for v1 app list

After the fast data sweep, select approximately 20–30 schools.

The v1 list should include:

* At least 4 NJ/local value options
* At least 3 regional public options
* At least 4 private/pre-health/STEM options
* At least 5 elite/reach options
* At least 3 BS/MD or early-assurance examples
* At least 3 nursing/PA/healthcare-flex examples

A school earns a spot if it teaches a useful strategic tradeoff.

Examples:

* Rutgers NB: NJ public flagship / value premed anchor
* TCNJ: smaller NJ public / value / BS-MD relevance
* Rowan: practical NJ healthcare ecosystem option
* NJIT: STEM/health-tech and BS-MD relevance
* Stevens: high-ROI STEM fallback, less classic premed
* Case Western: private pre-health ecosystem near major hospitals
* Pitt: urban research/clinical ecosystem
* Drexel/Temple: urban clinical exposure, practical pre-health context
* Hopkins: elite biomedical prestige
* Princeton/Penn/Duke/Brown/Cornell: elite/reach comparison set
* Sophie Davis: direct medicine / public mission-driven path

- Compare against TCNJ, Rowan, NJIT, and Case Western.
